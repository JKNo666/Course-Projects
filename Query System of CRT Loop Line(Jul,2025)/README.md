#Introduction
This is a query system where passengers can get the information of single stations,schedules of first&last trains as well as transfer trains,and the informations about which route the passenger could choose to get to the destinations.
#Questions:
This is Chongqing Rail Transit(CRT) Loop Line.There are 33 stations in operation(by Mar 2026).The stations are divided into local stations,where only local train stops,and express stations,where both local and express train are accessible.
Only one line is considered.
The questions are as follows.
(i)Given:The start and the destination on the loop line.Which is the shortter route of the journey,inner loop(Train runs Clockwise) or the outer loop(Train runs Counter-closkwise)?
(ii)Can the passenger take the express train to the destination?
(iii)Given:the schedules of the train departure time.Is the station chosen by user a local station or an express one?When does the train arrive at the station,and what are the stations nearby?
(iv)Is the station selected by user a transfer station?If so, show the schedule(s) of other lines.
